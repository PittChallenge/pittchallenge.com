This is our challenge to you.

We want everyone — pharmacy students, computer science majors, designers, and more — to come and help solve some of the biggest challenges facing the world today. Every week we will be putting up a video explaining a major problem in layman's terms. Join us on January 27-28th to solve these problems with faculty, alumni, and your peers.

Registration is free! We will provide free meals and swag, all you need to bring is your ideas and any tools you want to implement those ideas.
